Disaster Financial Assistance (DFA)

After a disaster, the provincial government may declare the event eligible for Disaster Financial Assistance (DFA). On this page you will be able to access the correct portal for your application type.

Learn more about DFA

Choose your application type below to begin.

Applications must be submitted within 90 days of the disaster being declared.

Individuals and Organizations

Private sector applicant may receive DFA assistance to restore uninsurable losses critical to homes, livelihoods, or community services.
